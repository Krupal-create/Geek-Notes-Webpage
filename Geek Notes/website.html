      This is a android based application using python kivy called Geek Notes. This application is basically a electronic study material provider for engineering students, who are facing problem with notes and questiion paper and many more updates. Even this application provides video playlist based on the subjects and each and everyone who uses this application can give feedback regarding this application.

  The application mainly consists of 4 screens:

  1. Home Screen :

  This screen consists of 3 categories Exam time tables, Question papers, Labratory manuals. In exam table section the user the get updates regarding the time table of each and every semester of all branch, and in question paper section the user can get previous year question paper of every semester and all branch, Laboratory manuals will be uploaded in further updates.   2. Notes Screen:

  This screen consists of 2 categories 2018 Scheme, 2021 Scheme, we are thinking of uploading 2022 scheme instead of 2021 scheme.   3. Video Screen:

  This screen consists videos of each and every semester of all branch, on one click that page will be redirected to YouTube playlist one of the best and most viewed and liked by many users or people.
